# Brazilian E-commerce Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes the performance of a Brazilian e-commerce company between 2016 and 2018.

The goal was to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights using Power BI, SQL and Excel.

The dashboard focuses on revenue, customer behavior, operational performance and product analysis.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI
- SQL
- Excel

---

## 📈 Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Customers
- Avg Ticket
- Revenue Growth %
- Avg Delivery Time
- Review Rate %
- Revenue per Customer

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Overview
High-level performance including revenue trends, top categories and geographic distribution.

### 2. Orders
Analysis of order volume, delivery performance and operational efficiency.

### 3. Customers
Customer behavior, retention analysis and satisfaction metrics.

### 4. Products
Product performance, category revenue, pricing and logistics cost analysis.

### 5. Insights & Recommendations
Summary of key findings and business recommendations.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Revenue increased significantly between 2016 and 2018
- Most customers are one-time buyers (low retention)
- São Paulo generates the majority of revenue
- Health & Beauty and Watches & Gifts are the top categories
- Logistics (freight) costs reduce profitability in some categories

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Improve customer retention through loyalty campaigns
- Optimize logistics costs
- Expand into underperforming regions
- Reduce dependency on a few categories and states
